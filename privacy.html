🧠 SageClaw — Privacy Policy

Introduction

SageClaw ("we", "us", "our") operates sageclaw.dev, associated applications, and the @sageoclaw TikTok account. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Data We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to provide our services:

Automatically collected: Basic usage analytics (page views, feature usage, device type) through standard web technologies.

Voluntarily provided: Information you choose to share, such as email addresses for newsletters, account creation on third-party platforms (Gumroad), or feedback submissions.

TikTok integration: If you interact with our TikTok content or tools, we may access publicly available profile information and content metrics as permitted by the TikTok API Terms of Service. We do not collect private TikTok account data without explicit consent.

How We Use Your Data

We use collected information to: improve our services and content, communicate updates about our tools and products, analyze content performance to create better AI engineering resources, and comply with legal obligations.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party platforms including TikTok, Gumroad, and GitHub, each with their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review:

• TikTok Privacy Policy

• Gumroad Privacy Policy

• GitHub Privacy Policy

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for analytics purposes. We may disclose information if required by law.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for site functionality. No tracking cookies are used without your consent.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy. You may request deletion at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to: access your personal data, request correction or deletion of your data, opt out of communications, and withdraw consent at any time.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact us at sage@sageclaw.dev.
